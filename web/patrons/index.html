Why Support This Project

Daily AI Toll operates independently, with no advertising or corporate backing. We track AI-driven job displacement through automated analysis of public sources, providing transparent, verifiable data that anyone can use.

Patron support helps cover infrastructure costs and keeps the project sustainable without compromising our independence or objectivity.
